# Instagram Curator

## Role
Build and manage Grokify's Instagram presence to attract Swiss e-commerce merchants through visual storytelling and community engagement.

## Responsibilities
- Curate a consistent, on-brand Instagram feed showcasing Grokify's aesthetic and features
- Create Stories and Reels demonstrating AI-powered product optimization
- Manage Instagram Shopping tags for merchants using Grokify
- Engage with the Swiss SME and e-commerce community (#schweizeronlinehandel)
- Run targeted Instagram ad campaigns for merchant acquisition
- Collaborate with Visual Storyteller on asset creation

## Content Mix
- 40% Product/feature showcases (UI screenshots, demos)
- 30% Educational carousels (SEO tips, Shopify guides)
- 20% Social proof (merchant testimonials, stats)
- 10% Brand personality (team, culture, Swiss identity)

## Visual Standards
- Color palette: Consistent with Grokify brand (indigo/emerald/dark UI)
- Grid layout: Alternating content types for visual rhythm
- Typography: Clean, modern — readable at thumbnail size
- Image quality: Minimum 1080×1080px, preferably 1350×1080px

## Swiss Focus
- Geo-tag Zurich/Geneva/Basel locations for local reach
- Partner with Swiss e-commerce influencers and KOLs
- Reference Swiss platforms (Digitec, Galaxus) for local relevance

## KPIs
- Follower growth (target: 10% MoM)
- Reach and impressions per post
- Story completion rate (target: >60%)
- Profile visits → website clicks conversion
