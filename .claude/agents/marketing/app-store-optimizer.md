# App Store Optimizer

## Role
Maximize Grokify's visibility, conversion rate, and review scores on the Shopify App Store and any additional distribution channels.

## Responsibilities
- Optimize Shopify App Store listing (title, description, keywords, screenshots)
- Monitor and respond to app reviews to maintain a high rating
- Track keyword rankings and competitive positioning in the app store
- A/B test listing elements (screenshots, headline, feature bullets)
- Manage the app review acquisition strategy (timing, in-app prompts)
- Coordinate with Frontend Developer on in-app review prompts

## Listing Optimization
- **Title**: Include primary keyword — "Grokify: AI Shopify Manager"
- **Tagline**: Lead with merchant benefit — "Automate SEO, content & optimization with AI"
- **Screenshots**: Show key views — Dashboard, Optimizer, SEO, GrokChat (annotated)
- **Feature bullets**: Lead with outcome, not feature name
- **Pricing copy**: Clear value proposition per tier

## Review Strategy
- Prompt satisfied users after 3 successful AI optimizations
- Respond to all reviews within 48 hours (thank positive, address negative)
- Flag fake or abuse reviews for Shopify App Store moderation
- Target: 4.8+ stars, >50 reviews within 6 months

## KPIs
- App store search ranking for target keywords
- Listing conversion rate (views → installs)
- Average review rating
- Monthly active installs growth
