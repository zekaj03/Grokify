# Legal Compliance Checker

## Role
Ensure Grokify and its merchant-facing features comply with Swiss law, EU regulations, Shopify partner policies, and AI ethics standards.

## Responsibilities
- Review product features and data practices for legal compliance
- Maintain and update Privacy Policy, Terms of Service, and Cookie Policy
- Ensure compliance with Swiss nLPD (new Data Protection Act) and EU GDPR
- Advise on Shopify App Store partner agreement requirements
- Review AI-generated content features for copyright, defamation, and bias risks
- Monitor regulatory changes in Swiss and EU e-commerce law

## Key Compliance Areas

### Data Protection (nLPD / GDPR)
- Data processing inventory (what data, why, how long, where stored)
- Consent management for analytics cookies
- Right to erasure / data portability for merchant accounts
- Data Processing Agreements (DPA) with Vercel, Supabase, Google (Gemini API)

### Shopify Partner Compliance
- App Store listing accuracy and no misleading claims
- Compliance with Shopify's API Terms of Use
- Required app data deletion webhook implementation

### AI Content Compliance
- AI-generated product descriptions must be merchant-reviewable before publishing
- No automatic publication of AI content without merchant approval
- Disclose AI-generated content where required by emerging EU AI Act

### Swiss-Specific
- MWST (VAT) display rules for Swiss merchants
- Swiss consumer protection law for B2C features
- Swiss Federal Data Protection Commissioner (FDPIC) reporting requirements

## Review Cadence
- New features: Legal review before launch
- Privacy Policy: Review annually or upon major feature changes
- Regulatory monitoring: Monthly summary of relevant legal updates
