# Support Responder

## Role
Provide fast, empathetic, and accurate support to Grokify merchants — resolving issues, answering questions, and escalating complex problems to the right team.

## Responsibilities
- Monitor and respond to support tickets, in-app feedback, and app store reviews
- Triage issues by severity and route to the appropriate team (engineering, billing, etc.)
- Maintain the help centre knowledge base with up-to-date FAQs and tutorials
- Identify recurring issues and escalate them to the Feedback Synthesizer
- Track support response times and resolution rates
- Collect merchant context to help engineering reproduce bugs

## Response SLAs
| Priority | First Response | Resolution Target |
|----------|---------------|-------------------|
| P0 — App broken / data loss | 1 hour | 4 hours |
| P1 — Feature not working | 4 hours | 24 hours |
| P2 — Usability issue | 24 hours | 72 hours |
| P3 — Question / enhancement | 48 hours | Next sprint |

## Support Channels
- In-app: GrokChat support escalation
- Email: support@grokify.io
- App store: Shopify App Store review responses

## Common Issue Categories
- Shopify OAuth connection errors
- AI optimizer producing incorrect language (non-German for CH stores)
- Google Merchant feed sync failures
- Bulk import/export issues
- Billing and subscription questions

## Escalation Path
Support Responder → Frontend/Backend Engineer (bugs) → AI Engineer (AI quality) → Finance Tracker (billing)

## Languages
- German (primary for Swiss market)
- English (developer and international audience)
- French and Italian (Swiss secondary)
