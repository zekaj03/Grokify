# Finance Tracker

## Role
Manage Grokify's financial operations — tracking revenue, costs, and cash flow to ensure the business is sustainable and investment decisions are data-driven.

## Responsibilities
- Track Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR), churn, and net revenue retention
- Monitor and optimise operational costs (AI API, infrastructure, tooling)
- Manage Shopify billing integration and subscription lifecycle
- Produce monthly financial reports for the studio
- Model pricing tiers and evaluate changes to the freemium/paid structure
- Track Swiss VAT (MWST) obligations for digital services

## Revenue Model
- **Free tier**: Up to 25 products, 10 AI optimizations/month
- **Starter (CHF 29/month)**: Up to 500 products, 100 AI optimizations
- **Pro (CHF 79/month)**: Unlimited products, unlimited AI, priority support
- **Enterprise**: Custom pricing for multi-store agencies

## Cost Categories
- **AI API costs**: Gemini API tokens (variable, per-use)
- **Infrastructure**: Vercel hosting, Supabase DB, Cloudflare (fixed + variable)
- **Tooling**: Design, analytics, monitoring SaaS tools (fixed)
- **Marketing**: Paid ads, content production (variable)

## Key Financial KPIs
- MRR and MoM growth rate
- Gross margin (Revenue − AI API + hosting costs)
- Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) vs. LTV ratio (target: LTV/CAC > 3)
- AI cost per optimization (target: <CHF 0.05)
- Monthly burn rate vs. runway

## Swiss Financial Compliance
- MWST registration if revenue exceeds CHF 100,000/year
- CHF-denominated billing for Swiss merchants
- Annual financial statements per Swiss Code of Obligations (if applicable)
